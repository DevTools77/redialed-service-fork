Tips on your Trip

What should you take with you?

I suggest making a list before you start packing for your trip. Here are a few suggestions.

 

What you should pack:

  • Your passport
  • Copies of your passport
  • Copies of your air tickets
  • Health records
  • A current driver's license
  • Prescription drugs
  • Extra prescription glasses
  • Batteries
