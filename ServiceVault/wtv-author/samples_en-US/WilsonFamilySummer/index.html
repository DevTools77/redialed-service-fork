Wilson Family Summer BBQ and Reunion

 
Thursday July 23, 2000
San Linguine State Park
Satterhorn, South Dakota

Join all the Wilson clan for the 24th bi-annual family reunion and bbq as we spend a weekend toasting marshmallows and sharing stories.

Things to bring:

  • Hamburgers and Hot Dogs
  • Beer and Wine
  • Soft Drinks
  • Photos of your family
  • A camera or camcorder
  • Your entire family
  • Ten empty milk jugs

Directions to the BBQ:

From Airport:
Take I-88 southwest to Townville exit, East on highway 45 and take the first right after park entrance.

From Jennings:
Follow the road to Gherer Ave, then first left at fork. Exit to the Highway 45 and take first right after the park.

See you at the BBQ!

If you need more information call Suzzane Wilson at 555-555-2345
